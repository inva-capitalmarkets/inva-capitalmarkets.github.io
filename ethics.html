Ethics and integrity

Ethics of the Company

At INVA, we are steadfast in our commitment to conducting all business activities with the highest ethical and professional standards. Our reputation for integrity is one of our most valuable assets, and maintaining this trust is fundamental to our operations. INVA has zero tolerance for unethical practices, ensuring that all interactions—whether with public officials, private individuals, or organizations—are conducted with honesty, transparency, and respect.

We prohibit any actions that could compromise our principles of fairness and accountability, including any improper behavior that undermines trust or integrity. Our commitment extends across all levels of the organization, fostering a culture that prioritizes ethical conduct, mutual respect, and adherence to applicable laws and regulations. By embedding ethics and integrity into every aspect of our business, we build enduring relationships with our stakeholders and maintains our position as a responsible and trustworthy partner.
