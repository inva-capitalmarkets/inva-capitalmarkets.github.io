Expanding Excellence Through Innovation

At Inva Capital Markets, we are expanding our vision beyond investments into innovative new ventures and brands that redefine excellence. SYLQ: Our Luxury Venture

Luxury isn’t just about what you own—it’s about how it makes you feel. At SYLQ, we create timeless pieces that speak to those who appreciate the finer things in life, without the need for excess. From fragrances that leave a lasting impression to fashion, accessories, and lifestyle essentials crafted with meticulous precision, every detail is designed to embody quiet sophistication.

Rooted in India, inspired by the world, SYLQ is where craftsmanship meets innovation. We source only the finest materials, ensuring each creation is an experience—elegant, refined, and built to last. True luxury isn’t about being seen; it’s about knowing you have the best.

SYLQ is more than a brand—it’s a statement. A way of life where prestige is effortless, and excellence is non-negotiable.
